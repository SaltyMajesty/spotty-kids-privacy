Privacy Policy

Spotty Kids
Last updated: May 2026

Summary: Spotty Kids does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app is designed with children's safety and privacy as our top priority.

Welcome

Welcome to Spotty Kids! This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our app. We take your privacy seriously, especially since our app is designed for children.

Information We Collect

Spotty Kids does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration or login
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal data
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • The app works completely offline for gameplay

Advertising

Our app displays advertisements from Meta Audience Network to support free access to the app. These ads:

  • May use device identifiers to show relevant content
  • Are displayed between gameplay sessions
  • Do not collect personal information from users

We do not have access to any data that may be collected by the ad network. For more information about Meta's data practices, please visit Meta's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy. Our practices include:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • The app is designed to be safe and fun for kids of all ages
  • No in-app purchases or external links that could lead children away from the app
  • No social features or user-generated content

Data Storage

All game data (such as scores and progress) is stored locally on your device only. We do not:

  • Transfer any data to external servers
  • Create user accounts or profiles
  • Store any data in the cloud

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet: Required for displaying advertisements
  • Network State: Used to check connectivity for ads

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in app updates and on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
